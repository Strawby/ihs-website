Plan Your Soak

Everything you need to enjoy the outdoor cedar tubs, from reservations to on-site amenities.

Crafted from aromatic cedar and continuously supplied with flowing mineral water, our outdoor tubs provide a deeply grounding soak set against a beautiful mountainside view. Privacy fencing creates a secluded haven while the open sky above brings crisp air and calm.

Stretch out, breathe deep, and let the cedar and minerals work their quiet magic. Each reservation gives you a dedicated tub with fresh water, comfortable seating, and access to nearby amenities for changing and relaxing between soaks.

  • Reservations required; weekend and evening slots fill quickly
  • Each tub is fully drained, cleaned, and refilled between sessions
  • Designed for small groups; please keep conversations low to honor the tranquil setting
  • Bring sandals or robes for the short outdoor walk to your tub
  • Outdoor experience in all seasons—pack for current weather
  • No glass or coolers permitted; visit our dining hall for snacks and drinks
  • Towels available to rent for $5; key locks available for $4
  • Arrive 15 minutes early to check in and review guidelines with staff
