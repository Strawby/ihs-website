Birch Bath

The largest of our Woodland Hallway baths, Birch offers an open, airy layout perfect for a more spacious soak. With two built-in bench areas and extra room to move, it’s the only Woodland Bath that can comfortably accommodate up to four guests. While the finishes remain consistent across the hallway, Birch stands apart for its generous size and gentle flow, creating a grounded yet expansive retreat.

Cottonwood Bath

With its long, rectangular layout and deeper pool, Cottonwood offers a spacious and immersive soaking experience. It comfortably fits up to three guests, making it a great option for a small group or anyone who enjoys room to stretch out. The added depth provides a calming, enveloping feel, though it may be less ideal for those with limited mobility. Peaceful and minimal, this bath invites you to fully relax and unwind.

Douglas Fir Bath

Douglas Fir offers a more grounded soak with a shallower pool depth, making it ideal for those who prefer a half-body immersion. Its layout is open enough to fit up to three guests, while still maintaining a sense of calm and intimacy. Gentle and easy to settle into, it’s a great choice for those who want a relaxed, social soak without the depth of a full plunge.

Evergreen Bath

Evergreen is our coziest Woodland Bath, intimate, quiet, and just the right size for two. Its smaller footprint creates a sense of calm and closeness, making it perfect for couples or solo guests seeking a peaceful, tucked-away retreat. Simple, soothing, and easy to settle into, Evergreen invites you to let go and stay present.
