Plan Your Soak

Everything you need to enjoy the outdoor cedar tubs, from reservations to on-site amenities.

Crafted from aromatic cedar, these tubs are built for both comfort and wellness. Cedar’s natural resistance to mold and mildew ensures a clean, fresh environment, while its soothing scent calms the mind and enhances every breath. Known for anti-inflammatory properties and support for respiratory health, cedar transforms a simple soak into a restorative ritual. Each tub is designed with extra depth and double the volume of our standard tubs, giving you more room to stretch out and fully unwind. A continuous flow of fresh mineral water keeps your soak pristine, while the cedar-wrapped cascade adds both beauty and function. And if you’re seeking the extreme renewal of hot and cold, our newest addition brings that option below.
