Plan Your Soak

Everything you need to enjoy a mud soak, from reservations to on-site amenities.

You might not be the kind of person who’d normally consider rubbing mud all over yourself. We promise this isn’t just ordinary dirt. Thanks to the waters of the hot springs, our mud is mineral-rich clay that naturally absorbs toxins from your pores as it dries. Whether you slather it on from head to toe or tastefully give yourself a facial, your skin will feel so soft and smooth after a rinse in the showers that you might even be tempted to tell strangers to give it a feel. We promise you’ll have a whole new appreciation of mud after your visit.

  • Reservations required on weekends; weekday visits are first-come, first-served
  • Each visit includes time to apply the mud, let it dry, and rinse in mineral-water showers
  • Arrive 15 minutes early to check in, hydrate, and change before your time slot begins
  • Swimsuits or provided wraps are required—keep in mind the clay can stain light fabrics
  • Towels available to rent for $5; key locks available for $4
  • For everyone’s comfort, please silence phones and enjoy the quiet atmosphere
