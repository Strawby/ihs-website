Perks with every stay

Every guest on the reservation receives access to our mineral waters and exclusive savings during your visit.

  • Full weekday access to our mineral water pool.
  • Weekend guests receive one complimentary time slot in our main mineral pool per day (Cabin guests: complimentary time slot is for up to 2 guests).
  • 20% off all water amenities, including Women’s & Men’s Caves (ages 18+), Private Baths, and Outdoor Tubs.
  • 10% off gift shop purchases.
  • Exclusive discounts at local Idaho Springs restaurants, breweries, and shops.
  • Over $60 in added value per night.

Booking & Property Details

  • Must be 21+ to book, with valid ID and matching credit card at check-in.
  • Check-in: 3:00 PM – 9:00 PM (call ahead for late arrivals); Check-out: 11:00 AM (late check-out available for a fee, based on availability).
  • Rates based on double occupancy; additional guests are $45/night per person.
  • Taxes not included in listed rates.
  • No pets or emotional support animals allowed (ADA-approved service animals only).
  • All rooms are non-smoking and do not have air conditioning.
  • For group reservations, email: support@indianhotsprings.com

Cancellation Policy

  • Cancel at least 48 hours before check-in for a full refund of your deposit (example: cancel by Friday for a Sunday check-in).
  • Cancel 24–48 hours before check-in for a full credit valid for 6 months.
  • Same-day cancellations will forfeit the deposit as a cancellation fee.
  • Need to make changes? Contact us and we’ll do our best to assist.
