Plan Your Soak

Everything you need to enjoy a mud soak, from reservations to on-site amenities.

Our Mud Room brings the natural benefits of mineral-rich clay directly to the skin. Gently warmed and deeply detoxifying, the mud draws out impurities while soothing muscles and improving circulation. As you rest in the quiet heat, tension dissolves and your body feels light, balanced, and refreshed.

Staff will guide you through the full experience—from applying the clay to rinsing off and rehydrating. Give yourself time to settle in, breathe deeply, and let the heat and minerals do their work.

  • Reservations required on weekends; walk-ins welcomed on weekdays as space allows
  • Average temperature 102–108°F, varies with geothermal flow
  • A minimal amount of chlorine is used to ensure safety
  • Arrive 15 minutes early to review guidelines and hydrate
  • Bring a swimsuit or wear provided wraps; mud may discolor light fabrics
  • Towels available to rent for $5; key locks available for $4
  • Please store phones and cameras—the room is a quiet, screen-free space
