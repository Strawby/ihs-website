Plan Your Soak

Everything you need to enjoy the dome-covered mineral pool, from reservations to on-site amenities.

You may think that it's impossible to have a tropical getaway in the middle of the mountains. Thanks to our staff botanist, and a magical sparkling dome, the main swimming pool gives off tropical vacation vibes all year long.

The hot mineral waters have been used to heal for centuries and you'll feel the benefits with whatever soaking style you choose—whether it's a slow paddle, a steady walk, or claiming a spot on one of the many built-in benches. While you're letting the tropical turquoise waters work their wonders, make sure to gaze around the edge and marvel at the banana trees, ferns, and flowers—many of which you won't find anywhere else in Colorado.

  • Weekdays – No reservation required
