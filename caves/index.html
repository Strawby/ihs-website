Men's & Women's Caves

Are you dying to have an adventure but also, whew, adventures kind of sound like a lot of work? The pools in our caves will make you feel like you’re traversing through time and connecting with the earth—minus the effort of spelunking.

The cave passages were driven into the rock in the early 1900s to create this underground oasis that’s a designated quiet zone to ensure relaxation and reflection for each visitor. Each walk-in tub allows you to study the nooks and crannies in the surrounding cave walls, and each area is gender specific.
